Our Workshop

A Partner of the Columbia Missourian

At the Missouri Urban Journalism Workshop, high school students from around the country work together with the guidance of professional journalists to produce stories, photos and broadcasts.

Celebrating its 46th year, the Missouri Urban Journalism Workshop welcomed 20 students from six U.S. states and Sweden to the School of Journalism for a seminar that immerses them in the Missouri Method last year. Over the course of the nine-day workshop, the students reported and wrote about the divisions facing the nation in this presidential election year.

Discover the fast-paced world of professional journalism during this 7-day Missouri Urban Journalism Workshop. You will produce stories in a professional setting including the Columbia Missourian newspaper and KOMU-TV, the local NBC affiliate, as well as across multiple media platforms. Your instruction will be guided by faculty experts at the Missouri School of Journalism and industry professionals.
